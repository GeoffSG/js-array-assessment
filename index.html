Random Image Picker

This is a showcase of utilising to fetch data (an image) from an external source, using an API.

  1. Enter an email address to the bottom textbox.
  2. Generate an image you wish to assign to that email address. You must have an email selected in the list to save an image to it.
  3. You may create another email address and set images to that, by selecting, or searching for one in the upper search bar.
